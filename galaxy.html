GALAXIES

➢ Our Galaxy :

Our galaxy is called the Milky Way. It is a large spiral galaxy made up of billions of stars, planets, gas, and dust. The Milky Way is about 100,000 light-years wide and contains more than 100 billion stars, including our Sun. Our solar system lies in a spiral arm known as the Orion Arm. At the center of the galaxy is a supermassive black hole called Sagittarius A*. The Sun takes about 225 to 250 million years to complete one full orbit around the Milky Way. 🌌
